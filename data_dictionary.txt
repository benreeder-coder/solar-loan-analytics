DATA DICTIONARY: solar_loan_delinquency.csv

One row per funded residential solar loan, Jan 2024 – Dec 2025.
Approximately 2,450 loans across 10 states and 5 installer partners.

COLUMNS:

loan_id                                 Unique loan identifier (e.g. L0001)
origination_date                        Date the loan was funded (YYYY-MM-DD)
state                                   Two-letter state code for the installation
credit_tier                             Borrower credit tier at origination: A (prime), B (near-prime), C (subprime)
loan_amount                             Loan principal in USD
system_size_kw                          Installed solar system size in kilowatts
loan_term_months                        Repayment term in months
interest_rate                           Annual interest rate (e.g. 5.25 = 5.25%)
monthly_payment                         Monthly loan payment in USD
installer_partner                       Name of the solar installation company
cents_per_watt_of_solar_electricity     Estimated cost of electricity from the solar system, in cents per watt
cents_per_watt_of_utility_electricity   Average local utility electricity cost, in cents per watt
is_delinquent                           TRUE if the loan is 30+ days past due
days_past_due                           Number of days past due (0 if current)
